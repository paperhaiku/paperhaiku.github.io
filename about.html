About
Vision
Make it easier to keep up with and understand deep learning research, for everyone.
Mission
Explain the core concept of each research paper as a small and elegant haiku so that the reader can have a basic understanding and knows what to expect when she/he starts reading the paper in full detail.
Strategy
Publish a handful of haikus everyday on research papers from different research areas of the deep learning field.
Contribution
All contributions are always welcome.

If you would like to publish a paper haiku, please send the haiku, paper link, relevant snippets from the paper to justify the haiku and tags relevant to the paper to paperhaiku@gmail.com
Also do let us know if you would like your name/pseudonym to be published with the haiku.
We also accept any feedback, suggestions and corrections on existing haikus in the same way.

Thank you.
